Miami, FL · DJ · Producer

Adam
Bogdan

Miami-based DJ and producer Adam Bogdan has spent nearly 20 years behind the decks — starting his journey in Romania before carving a path through Germany, Ibiza, and landing at the heart of the Miami underground scene.

A no-boundary artist with a wide musical vision, he has played for crowds across Europe, the Caribbean, and the USA — as both guest and resident — at venues including Fontainebleau, Arkadia Day Club, The Anderson, and Mode Miami.

His sound moves through house, minimal tech, and deep grooves, sprinkled with dub textures and vocal elements. The throughline is always the same: groove, first and always.
