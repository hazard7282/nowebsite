Getting Your Track Placed

How to Submit

Three ways to get your deep tech, minimal house, or underground electronic track in front of Adam. All submissions are listened to personally.

01
Choose Your Platform

Submit via PlaylistPush, SubmitHub, or Groover. Each platform has its own submission flow — create a free account, upload your track link, and target Adam Bogdan as a curator.

02
Match the Genre

Playlists focus on deep tech house, minimal house, underground house, groovy house, and rominimal. Tracks should be between 120–128 BPM with strong groove elements. Commercial or progressive house is outside scope.

03
Get a Response

Every paid submission on all three platforms receives a genuine response — either playlist placement with feedback, or a short honest note on why it wasn't the right fit. No ghost listens. Quality submissions may also be forwarded to Rapture's Deli for label consideration.

Who Is Adam Bogdan as a Spotify Curator?

Adam Bogdan is a Miami-based DJ, producer, and independent Spotify playlist curator with over 20 years of experience in underground electronic music. Born in Romania and based in Miami, Florida, Adam's curatorial perspective is rooted in the same sounds he plays and releases — deep tech house, minimal house, rominimal, and groovy underground electronic music.

As the founder of Rapture's Deli, a Miami underground house label, Adam has a keen ear for what works in the underground: long rhythms, hypnotic basslines, well-placed vocals, and tracks that respect the dance floor. His playlists reflect that sensibility — no filler, no hype, just quality underground selections updated regularly.

What Genres Does This Curator Accept?

Adam Bogdan's Spotify playlists cover the following genres: deep tech house, minimal tech house, deep house, underground house, groovy house, dub house, vocal house, rominimal, and Romanian minimal. BPM range is typically 118–128. If your track could fit a late-night underground set in Miami or a basement club in Berlin, it belongs here.

Submitting via PlaylistPush, SubmitHub & Groover

PlaylistPush is ideal for artists who want guaranteed playlist placement consideration from independent curators. Tracks are reviewed within the platform, and curators like Adam receive tracks filtered by genre — so deep tech and minimal submissions go directly to the right ears.

SubmitHub allows artists to submit to playlist curators, music blogs, and labels with a credits system. Adam's SubmitHub profile accepts electronic, deep house, and tech house submissions and provides written feedback on every premium submission.

Groover is a Paris-based platform popular with European artists and labels. It connects tracks with curators, media, and labels — all with a response guarantee. Adam uses Groover to discover new releases from European underground artists, and occasionally forwards outstanding tracks to Rapture's Deli for label consideration.

Why Submit to an Independent Curator?

Independent Spotify curators like Adam Bogdan offer something algorithmic playlists can't: genuine taste, genre expertise, and a direct connection to a real audience. Playlist placement with an active independent curator helps your track reach underground house listeners who actually care about the music — not just passive listeners served by an algorithm. It also builds artist-curator relationships that can lead to label interest, remix opportunities, and real industry connections.
